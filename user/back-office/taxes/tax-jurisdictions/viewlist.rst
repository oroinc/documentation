View and Filter Tax Jurisdictions
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^

.. begin

To view all tax jurisdictions, navigate to **Taxes > Tax Jurisdictions** in the main menu.

.. image:: /user/img/taxes/tax_jurisdiction_all.png
   :alt: The page of all tax available tax jurisdictions

.. note:: To handle a big volume of data, use the page switcher, increase *View Per Page* or use filters to narrow down the list to just the codes you need.

In the tax jurisdictions list, you will find the information about the code (unique identifier), detailed description, and the dates when the tax jurisdiction was created and updated.

Hover over the |IcMore| **More Options** menu to the right of the item and click |IcView| to open its details page, |IcEdit| to edit, or  |IcDelete| to remove the tax jurisdiction.

.. stop
