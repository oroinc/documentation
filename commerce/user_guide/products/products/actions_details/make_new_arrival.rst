Add / Remove a Product from New Arrivals
========================================


Mark a Product as a New Arrival
-------------------------------

.. start_products_actions_new_arrival_mark

To mark a product as featured:

1. Navigate to the **Products > Products** using main menu.
#. Hover over the |IcMore| **More Options** menu to the right of the item and click the |IcEdit| **Edit** icon to start editing its details.
#. In the **General** section, set **Is New Arrival** to *Yes*.
#. Click **Save**.

.. stop_products_actions_new_arrival_mark

Remove a Product from New Arrivals
----------------------------------

.. start_products_actions_new_arrival_remove

To remove a product from Featured Products block, set **Is New Arrival** to *No* in the product details:

1. Navigate to the **Products > Products** using main menu.
#. Hover over the |IcMore| **More Options** menu to the right of the item and click the |IcEdit| **Edit** icon to start editing its details.
#. In the **General** section, set **Is New Arrival** to *No*.
#. Click **Save**.
